push(task)

work!()

Creates a new thread to process the Task queue. Reads one Task at a time from the queue and replicates the operation until success. If an exception is raised while the operation is replicated, an ErrorHandler will be created and called. After the ErrorHandler has finished, it will retry to replicate the operation.
